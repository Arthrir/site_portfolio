Profile & Ambitions

Currently an engineering student at Mines Saint-Étienne (ISMIN), I am building a profile at the intersection of engineering, design and management. My background in microelectronics and computer science has given me strong technical foundations through projects in embedded systems, hardware, processor architecture and artificial intelligence.

Over time, through both academic projects and student leadership experiences, I realized that what drives me most is not only the technical design of a system. I am equally interested in understanding why a technology is developed, for whom it creates value, and how it can be turned into a relevant, coherent and impactful product.

This perspective now shapes my academic and professional direction. In the long term, I aim to grow into Product Owner / Product Manager roles, at the crossroads of technology, user experience and strategy, with the ambition of leading the development of ambitious and high-impact technological products.

With this in mind, I want to complement my engineering education with a stronger focus on product vision, usage and experience, notably through a design semester at Politecnico di Milano, followed by a management specialization with emlyon business school. My ambition is to be able to work seamlessly with technical, business and design teams alike, in order to align product vision, technical feasibility and value creation.

My goal is to build a genuine triple competency across engineering, design and management, in order to design and lead innovative technological products with a vision that is both technical, human and strategic.
